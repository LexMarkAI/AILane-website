HOW IT WORKS
From raw tribunal data to a single number
The ACEI pipeline transforms unstructured regulatory activity into a deterministic, reproducible exposure score governed by a public constitutional framework.
01 — MONITOR

Automated Surveillance

Continuous scraping of GOV.UK employment tribunal decisions, statutory instruments, and enforcement bulletins. Every relevant signal captured in real time.

02 — CLASSIFY

12-Category Taxonomy

Each event is deterministically assigned to one of 12 constitutional categories using overlap allocation rules. No ambiguity, no miscellaneous bucket.

03 — SCORE

Mathematical Engine

Likelihood × Impact × Sector Multiplier × Jurisdiction Multiplier × Velocity Adjustment. Eight sequential computation steps, fully reproducible within 24 hours.

04 — QUANTIFY

Your ACEI Score

A single 0–100 number reflecting your aggregate regulatory exposure. Calibrated against Monte Carlo simulations. Bounded by constitutional shock caps and velocity controls.

INSTITUTIONAL GOVERNANCE
Built like a financial index, not a newsletter
The ACEI is governed by a published constitutional framework with formal amendment protocols, version control, and audit reproducibility — the same infrastructure that underpins indices like the FTSE and S&P.

Article I — Determinism

Identical inputs always produce identical outputs. Stochastic elements are prohibited in live scoring. Every weekly value is reproducible within 24 hours.

Article IV — Calibration

DomainMaxReference set at 95th percentile via Monte Carlo simulation (1,000+ runs). Prevents premature saturation and preserves structural escalation headroom.

Article V — Structural Shifts

Distinguishes ordinary volatility from fundamental regulatory transformation. Requires both quantitative threshold AND qualifying legal trigger. Shock Caps enforce bounded movement.

Article XII — Amendments

Major changes require 75% supermajority plus external advisory review. 24-month backtesting mandatory. Non-retroactive — historical scores are permanent.
