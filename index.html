How It Works
Three inputs. One constitutionally-governed score.
The ACEI calculates employer exposure using a deterministic formula defined in Article III of its founding constitution. Identical inputs always produce identical outputs.
01
📊

Event Volume Index (EVI)

Tracks weekly tribunal decision volumes per category against 52-week empirical baselines. A surge in discrimination claims in your sector triggers an immediate EVI spike — before it becomes a headline.

02
⚡

Enforcement Intensity Index (EII)

Monitors real-time enforcement actions from the EHRC, HSE, and ICO using 90-day rolling windows. When a regulator ramps up activity in your category, your EII responds within the week.

03
📜

Structural Change Index (SCI)

Maps legislative changes and regulatory updates to your exposure categories with impact-level scoring. The Employment Rights Act 2025, data protection amendments, working time reforms — all captured and weighted.

Lraw = (0.4 × EVI) + (0.3 × EII) + (0.3 × SCI)
Article III §3.2.1 — Likelihood Derivation Formula

Legislative lifecycle — how Ailane tracks it
01
Parliamentary Passage — SCI forward exposure flag raised
Bill enters the library. Significance rating assigned across affected ACEI categories. Forward Exposure Register updated. Clients notified at their tier before the bill becomes law.
02
Royal Assent — obligations mapped, SCI weight assigned
Act confirmed. Employer obligations extracted and structured. SCI significance score calculated. Commencement timeline tracked instrument by instrument.
03
Commencement — ACEI recomputed, Compliance Checker updated
Law enters force. Full ACEI SCI recalculation triggered. Compliance Checker requirement set updated — contracts assessed against the new obligation immediately. RRI readiness scoring activated.
04
Post-Commencement — tribunal signals feed EVI and EII
Tribunal decisions under the new law tracked weekly. First-wave enforcement signals feed back into Event Volume and Enforcement Intensity sub-indices. Score reflects real enforcement patterns, not just legislative text.

ACEI v1.0

Core Exposure Index

Quantifies regulatory exposure across 12 employment law categories using a three-component formula combining tribunal volumes, enforcement intensity, and structural legislative change.

  • 0–100 domain index scale
  • Deterministic computation (Art I §1.3)
  • Weekly calculation cycle
  • Full sub-index transparency

RRI v1.0

Regulatory Readiness Index

Assesses organisational preparedness through 5 pillars and a 9-attribute Evidence Impact Profile. Measures how well your governance, policies, and culture mitigate raw exposure identified by the ACEI.

  • 5 readiness pillars
  • 9-attribute evidence framework
  • Strict separation from ACEI
  • Self-assessment with verification
  • Mitigation offset computation
CCI v1.0

Compliance Conduct Index

Evaluates employer conduct using 6 weighted components with Bayesian credibility weighting. Tribunal claim frequency, adverse outcomes, payout severity, enforcement events, and repeat allegation patterns.

  • 6 weighted components
  • Bayesian credibility scoring
  • Per-employer conduct rating
  • Independent verification layer
  • Insurance-grade underwriting input
