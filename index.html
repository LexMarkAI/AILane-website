Institutional Governance
Built on constitutional
infrastructure
The ACEI isn't a black box. It's governed by a formal constitution with versioned methodology, amendment controls, and audit reproducibility.
Article I

Deterministic computation

Identical inputs produce identical outputs. No stochastic elements in live scoring. Every weekly index value is reproducible within 24 hours upon request.

Article IV

Calibrated against extremes

The Domain Max Reference is set at the 95th percentile of Monte Carlo simulated extreme exposure — ensuring the scale has structural headroom for genuine crises.

Article V

Structural regime controls

Shock caps prevent uncontrolled index escalation. Single-event movement cannot exceed 8 points. Weekly movement cannot exceed 25 under any circumstance.

Article XII

Version-controlled evolution

Major methodology changes require supermajority governance approval, 24-month backtesting, and 14-day review windows. Amendments apply prospectively only.
