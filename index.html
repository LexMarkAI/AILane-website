The Index Triad

Three indices. Complete compliance intelligence.

Each index is governed by its own founding constitution with institutional-grade methodology, version control, and formal governance structures.

📊

Core Exposure Index

ACEI · 12 CATEGORIES

Quantifies your regulatory exposure across 12 employment law categories. The headline number boards and investors understand — how exposed is this business right now?

Score range: 0–100 · Updated weekly
🛡️

Regulatory Readiness Index

RRI · 5 PILLARS · 9 EIP

Measures how prepared your organisation is to handle regulatory change. Evaluates policies, training, governance, monitoring, and response capability across 9 evidence-based indicators.

Score range: 0–100 · 5 readiness pillars
⚖️

Compliance Conduct Index

CCI · 6 COMPONENTS · BAYESIAN

Assesses your organisation's behavioural compliance through Bayesian credibility analysis. Tracks how well you translate readiness into actual compliant conduct over time.

Score range: 0–100 · Bayesian credibility
