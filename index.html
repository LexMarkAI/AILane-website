Constitutional Framework
Three Independently Governed Indices

Adverse Claim Exposure Index

DI = Σ(Cw·Cs) × SM × JM

Quantifies external employment claim exposure across 12 categories. Computed from 130,854+ classified tribunal decisions, sector multipliers, and jurisdictional intelligence.

Available from Operational Readiness tier

Regulatory Readiness Index

WDRS = Σ(Ps·Pv) × 20

Measures forward-looking organisational preparedness. 5 pillars, 9-attribute Evidence Impact Profile (EIP). Dual-track evidence model — Track A and Track B.

Governance tier and above

Compliance Conduct Index

CCI = Σ(Ci·Zi) ÷ Σ(Zi)

Assesses historical conduct posture across 6 components using Bayesian credibility weighting. Rewards consistent, documented compliance behaviour over time.

Governance tier and above

Each index is independently computed under its own founding constitution. No cross-contamination between indices. Strict separation doctrine enforced at the data layer.
