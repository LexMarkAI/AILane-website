First product: Ailane Compliance

A subscription service for businesses: regulation updates, AI summaries, and “what to do next” actions — tailored by jurisdiction and industry.

  • Monitor updates from regulators and public bodies.
  • Summarise impact in plain English (risk, cost, deadlines).
  • Recommend actions: checklist + policy templates + owner.
  • Deliver alerts by email + dashboard (audit trail).
  • Monetise via tiered subscription (SMB → Enterprise).
