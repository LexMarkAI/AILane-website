Requires Attention Holiday Pay Accrual
Working Time Regulations 1998, reg. 16
What we found

The holiday pay calculation method in this contract does not reflect the statutory entitlement for variable-hours workers. Workers on zero-hours arrangements are entitled to holiday pay calculated on a 12-week average of actual earnings, not a fixed weekly rate.

What this means

This clause may not meet current statutory requirements and may be worth discussing with an employment solicitor.

Requires Attention Notice Period
Employment Rights Act 1996, s.86
What we found

The notice period stated in this contract (one week) falls below the statutory minimum for a worker who has been employed continuously for two or more years.

What this means

Statutory notice entitlements increase with length of service. This clause may not reflect your statutory entitlement.
