Safeguarding Data-Handling Statement.

The Foundation works with children and young people, so Ailane holds itself to the strictest data posture in this room.

(1) No live personal data — and no children's data of any kind — is processed by this platform until a data protection impact assessment (UK GDPR Article 35) is complete and a data processing agreement is executed. Until then the room runs on synthetic or pseudonymised records only.

(2) When the safeguarding module is enabled, access is restricted, every access is logged, and safeguarding records are never used for any purpose beyond the register the trustees keep.

(3) Any incident touching safeguarding data is treated as severity-critical: the Foundation is notified without undue delay and supported in its own duties, including serious incident reporting to the Charity Commission.

(4) Beneficiary data never appears in any public material, case study or testimonial.

(5) Questions or concerns: partnerships@ailane.ai — or raise it on the Feedback page and it goes straight to the team.
