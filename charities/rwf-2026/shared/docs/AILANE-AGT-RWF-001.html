Founding Charity Partner Agreement

Founding Charity Partner Agreement between AI LANE LIMITED (Company No. 17035654; ICO Reg. No. 00013389720) ("Ailane") and THE RUDOLPH WALKER FOUNDATION (Registered Charity No. 1157221, 154 Wanstead Lane, Ilford, IG1 3SG) ("the Foundation").

1. Definitions: "Charity Suite" — Ailane's charity compliance intelligence platform (Obligations Calendar and Registers; Safeguarding Register; Event Compliance Pack; People and Volunteer Compliance; Trustee Governance Board); "Pilot Term" — six months from the Go-Live Date, extendable by written agreement; "Go-Live Date" — the date the Foundation's workspace is provisioned; "DPIA" — a data protection impact assessment under Article 35 UK GDPR.

2. Licence: fee-waived, non-exclusive, non-transferable use of the Charity Suite for the Foundation's own charitable operations during the Pilot Term, as first Preferred Charity Partner; the fee waiver is exclusive to charities so designated by Ailane's CEO; continuation beyond the Pilot Term is by separate written agreement.

3. Services and phase gates: Ailane provisions the workspace, seeds the obligations registers from verifiable public sources with provenance attached, operates reminder and escalation automation, and provides a monthly tier report; Phases 0–1 operate on synthetic or pseudonymised records and the Foundation's non-personal operational data only; the Safeguarding Register is enabled only after a completed DPIA and an executed data processing agreement; no children's personal data is processed before that gate, without exception; named operational contact each side, support responses within two working days.

4. The Foundation nominates a named contact, joins a monthly structured feedback session, contributes to the defect and feature log, and completes the trustee verification pass on seeded register rows; the Foundation remains solely responsible for its decisions, filings, reports and regulatory interactions — nothing transfers any trustee duty to Ailane.

5. Publicity: neither party names the other without prior written approval; any case study or testimonial requires the Foundation's written approval before publication; no beneficiary data, and no data relating to children, appears in any public material.

6. Data protection: each party complies with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018; where Ailane processes personal data on the Foundation's behalf it acts as processor under a data processing agreement executed before such processing begins; business contact details are processed by each party as independent controller; Ailane notifies the Foundation without undue delay of any personal data breach affecting its data and assists with the Foundation's own notification duties, including serious incident reporting to the Charity Commission.

7. Confidentiality: as per the Mutual NDA between the parties, which continues to apply.

8. Intellectual property: Ailane retains all rights in the Charity Suite; the Foundation retains all rights in its own data, used by Ailane only to provide the services; the Foundation grants a non-exclusive licence to use feedback and de-identified usage learnings to improve the Charity Suite; "Ai Lane" is a trade mark of AI Lane Limited (UK00004347220).

9. Nature of the service: the Charity Suite provides regulatory intelligence — it identifies, tracks, assembles and reminds; it does not provide legal advice, does not certify compliance, and does not substitute for qualified legal, safeguarding or accounting professionals; the trustees retain full accountability; platform disclaimers are not removed or obscured.

10. Liability: nothing excludes liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, fraud, or anything that cannot lawfully be excluded; subject to that, each party's aggregate liability is capped at £10,000, and neither party is liable for indirect or consequential loss, loss of profits, or loss of donations or grants.

11. Term and termination: from the date of last acceptance for the Pilot Term; either party on 30 days' written notice; immediately for unremedied material breach (14 days) or insolvency; Ailane may suspend the platform where reasonably necessary to protect data or beneficiaries pending investigation.

12. Exit: data returned or deleted at the Foundation's election within 30 days, deletion certified on request; clauses 5–10 and 12 survive.

13. General: entire agreement; written variation only; no partnership or agency; notices by email to named contacts; law of England and Wales, exclusive jurisdiction of its courts.

Execution: by in-room acceptance — each signatory, authenticated by their invited email, completes the acceptance step; the acceptance record is that party's signature, effective as a simple electronic signature under the law of England and Wales. For AI LANE LIMITED: Mark Lane-Cousin, Director. For THE FOUNDATION: an authorised trustee.
