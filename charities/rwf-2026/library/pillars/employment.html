Employment and volunteers — people, paid and unpaid

Why this matters to the Foundation

four registered volunteers, event staff at busy times — the line between volunteer and worker is drawn by reality, not labels, and crossing it accidentally creates back-pay and pension claims.

The duties
  • written particulars from day one for employees;
  • National Living Wage (£12.71 for 21+ from April 2026);
  • working time and rest;
  • right-to-work checks;
  • pensions auto-enrolment where eligible;
  • volunteers reimbursed for genuine expenses only — payment or fixed reward creates worker status;
  • health and safety duties extend to volunteers and the public.
What we watch

NMW/NLW upratings, Employment Rights Act amendments and new employment legislation as enacted, Equality Act guidance, HSE event guidance. The Library's "Employment contracts for charities" page assembles what an engagement should address — groundwork for your solicitor.
