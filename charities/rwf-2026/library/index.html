The nine source sets we watch for charities

These are the regulators and rule-makers whose changes matter to a charity like yours. We keep an eye on them so you do not have to.

1
Charity Commission guidance & alerts
Registration, reporting and trustee guidance for charities in England & Wales, including the CC-series publications.
Charity Commission for England & Wales
2
Charity legislation tracker
Charities Act 2011 and 2022 commencements and the statutory instruments that bring new duties into force.
legislation.gov.uk
3
Fundraising Regulator
Code of Fundraising Practice updates and the themes coming out of its adjudications.
Fundraising Regulator
4
ICO for charities
Data-protection guidance, registration fees and enforcement themes that touch charity operations.
Information Commissioner's Office
5
HMRC Charities
Gift Aid, reliefs and record-keeping updates for recognised charities.
HM Revenue & Customs
6
Employment law watch
Employment Rights Act developments, tribunal trends and written-particulars duties for charity employers — the Ailane home ground.
UK employment law
7
Safeguarding & DBS
Disclosure and Barring Service updates and the safeguarding expectations placed on trustees.
DBS · safeguarding guidance
8
The Pensions Regulator
Auto-enrolment duties, re-declaration cycles and small-employer guidance.
The Pensions Regulator
9
Companies House
Accounts and confirmation-statement requirements for charitable companies.
Companies House
