SECTION 5

Document Processing & Deletion

Where you use the Ailane Compliance Checker to upload employment contracts, staff handbooks, or other documents for analysis, the following specific provisions apply:

PROCESSING

Uploaded documents are transmitted over TLS-encrypted connections to our secure processing environment hosted on Supabase (EU-Central-1, Frankfurt). The document content is passed to an AI language model (Anthropic Claude API) solely for the purpose of performing compliance analysis against our Knowledge Library of legislative instruments.

DELETION

The original document file is permanently and irrecoverably deleted from our storage infrastructure immediately upon completion of analysis. Deletion is automatic and irreversible. We do not retain backups of uploaded documents. The timestamp of deletion is recorded in our system logs.

WHAT IS RETAINED

Following deletion of the document, we retain only: the document’s filename, file size, document type, submission timestamp, and the structured analysis output (findings, scores, statutory references). This structured output contains no direct quotation of document content beyond brief clause excerpts included in the report delivered to you.

THIRD-PARTY AI PROCESSING

Document content is transmitted to Anthropic, Inc. via their API solely for analysis. Anthropic acts as a data processor on our behalf. Anthropic’s API usage policies prohibit training on API-submitted content. For Anthropic’s privacy policy, see anthropic.com/privacy.

If you believe a document you uploaded contained personal data relating to identifiable individuals (e.g. a named employee’s employment contract), we confirm that such data was deleted in the process described above and is no longer held by Ailane.

SECTION 9

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data. These rights are not absolute and may be subject to conditions or exemptions in specific circumstances.

Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether we process your personal data and, if so, a copy of that data together with supplementary information about our processing.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to require us to correct inaccurate personal data and to complete incomplete personal data without undue delay.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data where it is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, where consent is withdrawn, or where processing is unlawful, subject to our legal retention obligations.

Right to Restriction (Article 18)

You have the right to request that we restrict processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, for example where you contest the accuracy of data or object to processing pending verification of our legitimate grounds.

Right to Portability (Article 20)

Where processing is based on your consent or a contract and is carried out by automated means, you have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object at any time to processing based on legitimate interests, including profiling. You also have an absolute right to object to processing for direct marketing purposes.

Rights re: Automated Decisions (Article 22)

Where decisions are made solely by automated means that produce legal or similarly significant effects, you have the right to request human review and to contest the decision.

Right to Complain to the ICO

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113 if you believe we have processed your data unlawfully.

HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS: To exercise any of the rights above, please contact us at privacy@ailane.ai with the subject line “Data Subject Request.” We will respond within one calendar month of receiving your request. In complex cases, we may extend this period by a further two months, in which case we will notify you within the first month. We will not charge a fee for responding to a valid request unless the request is manifestly unfounded or excessive.

SECTION 11

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against accidental loss, unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit between your device and our servers
  • Encryption at rest for all data stored in our database infrastructure
  • Row-level security policies applied to all database tables containing personal data
  • JWT-based authentication with automatic token expiry for all user sessions
  • Isolated processing environment for document analysis — documents are not persisted to general storage
  • Strict access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure configuration and access policies
  • Secure deletion protocols for uploaded documents upon analysis completion

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by UK GDPR Article 33. Where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to you, we will also notify you directly without undue delay, as required by Article 34.

Notwithstanding our security measures, no internet transmission or electronic storage system is entirely secure. If you have reason to believe that your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@ailane.ai.
