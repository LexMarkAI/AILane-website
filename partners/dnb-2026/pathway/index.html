Engagement Pathway

A six-phase pathway from pre-engagement through to renewal cascade, with three asynchronous next-step paths to suit different counterparty preferences.

Six-phase summary

From AILANE-ROADMAP-DNB-001 v1.3 §3

Phase A — Roadmap & routing

The roadmap, commercial proposal, and Legal & Audit pack are released to the deal-room. Internal counterparty routing is acknowledged and the engagement record is opened.

Earliest: April 2026 · Envelope: April–May 2026

Phase B — NDA execution & Pilot SOW preparation

A mutual non-disclosure agreement is executed between AI Lane and Dun & Bradstreet. The Pilot Statement of Work is scoped against the per-package data field schedule. NDA execution unlocks subsequent document releases in this workspace.

Earliest: May 2026 · Envelope: May–June 2026

Phase C — Pilot SOW execution & pilot delivery

Pilot SOW signed; pilot fee received; per-CLID DPIA addendum executed; data exchange initiated. Pilot delivery proceeds against the field schedule and KPI definitions specified in the proposal.

Earliest: June 2026 · Envelope: June–August 2026

Phase D — Pilot complete & MCA negotiation

Pilot delivery completes; package selection is confirmed; Master Commercial Agreement negotiation opens under the 60-day negotiation lock-in. Package economics are finalised, including renewal cascade timing.

Earliest: August 2026 · Envelope: August–October 2026

Phase E — MCA execution & production rollout

Master Commercial Agreement executed; production data exchange begins; reconciliation reports and KPI dashboards activate. Director-level review checkpoints are scheduled at week 4 and week 12.

Earliest: October 2026 · Envelope: Q4 2026

Phase F — Renewal cascade

Anniversary renewal cascade across packages, with expansion options reviewed at each cycle. Renewal cadence and pricing review windows are anchored in the MCA.

Earliest: 2027 · Envelope: per renewal cycle

Three asynchronous next-step paths

From AILANE-ROADMAP-DNB-001 v1.3 §12

Path A — NDA-first

The conventional route: a mutual NDA is signed before substantive commercial detail is exchanged. Suits counterparties whose internal process requires NDA execution prior to any working session.

Path B — Pilot SOW direct

For counterparties willing to operate under the proposal's confidentiality covering terms, the Pilot Statement of Work can be the first executed document. NDA terms are absorbed into the SOW.

Path C — Q&A first

Substantive questions are routed via this deal-room or to partnerships@ailane.ai ahead of any document execution. Useful where internal review needs orientation before committing to a signing path.
