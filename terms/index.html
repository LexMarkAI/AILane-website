2. Services

Ailane provides a UK employment law compliance and regulatory intelligence platform. Our services include, depending on your subscription tier:

  • Employment contract and HR policy compliance analysis using the ACEI methodology.
  • Access to the Ailane Knowledge Library, a structured repository of UK employment legislation, regulations, and case law, with Eileen AI intelligence assistance.
  • The Regulatory Intelligence Ticker, delivering graded regulatory developments relevant to your sector and exposure profile.
  • Eileen persistent intelligence sessions — Onboarding Mode (profile assembly and initial orientation), Standard Mode (ongoing regulatory intelligence delivery), and Proactive Mode (autonomous regulatory alert generation) — available to Operational, Governance, and Institutional tier subscribers.
  • Access to enriched UK Employment Tribunal decision data, Health and Safety Executive (HSE) enforcement intelligence, Coroner Prevention of Future Death (PFD) reports, and regulatory intelligence from the Advisory, Conciliation and Arbitration Service (ACAS), His Majesty's Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS), and the Fair Work Agency (launched 7 April 2026).
  • Governance and Institutional tier subscribers additionally access Regulatory Risk Index (RRI) and Contextual Compliance Index (CCI) data, judicial pattern intelligence (JIPA — Judge Intelligence and Pattern Analysis), and multi-document portfolio analysis.
  • Legal partner referral services (see Section 8).

The specific features available to you depend on your active subscription tier. The Feature Matrix Tier Plan, published within the Platform, is the governing reference for tier-specific capabilities.

3. Nature of Platform

3.1 Regulatory Intelligence, Not Legal Advice

The Ailane platform provides regulatory intelligence. It does not provide legal advice. Nothing on the Platform — including Eileen's responses, compliance reports, ticker items, Knowledge Library content, or any other output — constitutes legal advice, legal opinion, or a substitute for advice from a qualified solicitor or barrister. You should always seek independent legal advice for decisions about your specific legal position.

Ailane identifies, quantifies, and contextualises employer regulatory exposure. The Platform informs your risk management. It does not advise you on what action to take, and it does not represent that any action you take based on Platform output will position you to demonstrate compliance with applicable law.

3.2 ACEI Methodology

Compliance scores generated by the Platform's ACEI engine are produced by a deterministic algorithmic process applied to the content of your documents and the applicable legislative framework. Scores reflect the Platform's assessment of regulatory exposure based on the data available at the time of analysis. Scores are not legal opinions and do not constitute a guarantee of any legal outcome. The ACEI methodology is subject to ongoing calibration; scores may change between analyses even where the underlying documents are unchanged.

3.3 Limitations of AI Intelligence

Eileen's intelligence outputs are generated by artificial intelligence and reflect the knowledge and data available to the Platform at the time of delivery. While we take significant steps to maintain accuracy — including sourcing from authoritative public sources and applying constitutional guardrails to every output — AI-generated intelligence may contain errors or omissions. You must exercise your own professional judgment in relation to all Platform outputs. Ailane accepts no liability for decisions made in reliance on Platform outputs without independent professional verification.

3.4 Early Access Designation

Certain Platform features may be designated as Early Access. Early Access features are fully operational but are subject to active development and calibration. Quality standards under our Quality Assurance Index (QAI) framework apply to Early Access features. Early Access designation will be clearly indicated within the Platform.

3.5 Voice and Vision Interactions

Governance and Institutional tier subscribers may access Eileen's voice interaction capability, powered by Google DeepMind's Gemini Live API. Voice interaction is a supplementary access modality — all intelligence delivered through voice is also available through text interaction. Voice interaction is opt-in and disabled by default. When using voice interaction, your speech audio is processed in real time and is not recorded or stored by Ailane. See our Privacy Policy (Section 6) for full details of voice data processing.

An optional vision feature allows Eileen to reference the current state of your dashboard screen during a voice session. Vision is separately opt-in and requires explicit screen sharing permission from you. Dashboard screen content is processed in real time and is not stored.

3.6 AI Provider Architecture

The Ailane Platform uses a dual-provider AI architecture:

  • Anthropic, Inc. (Claude AI) powers compliance analysis, Knowledge Library intelligence, and Eileen's persistent text intelligence sessions. Anthropic is the primary AI provider.
  • Google DeepMind (Gemini Live API) powers Eileen's real-time voice and vision interaction (Governance and Institutional tiers only).

Both providers operate under data processing agreements with Ailane, under which they are prohibited from using Platform data to train their models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

3.7 Persistent Intelligence Sessions New v4.0

Operational, Governance, and Institutional tier subscribers have access to Eileen's persistent intelligence sessions. Sessions are initiated on demand and operate with a 60-minute inactivity timeout. Session context — the conversational history and document context of an active session — does not persist beyond the session timeout. When you start a new session, Eileen rebuilds context from your stored regulatory profile; the text of prior sessions is not retained.

You may have one Standard Mode session and one Proactive Mode session active simultaneously. While an Onboarding Mode session is active for your account, Standard Mode sessions cannot be initiated concurrently. Onboarding Mode is the initial session state for new subscribers.

Session content transmitted to Anthropic's infrastructure during a session is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy and the applicable data transfer safeguards described therein.

3.8 Proactive Intelligence Delivery New v4.0

Proactive Mode is a contracted feature available to Operational, Governance, and Institutional tier subscribers. When Proactive Mode is enabled, Eileen may generate regulatory intelligence alerts autonomously — without requiring you to initiate a session — when regulatory developments intersect with your identified exposure profile. Proactive alerts are delivered to your Platform dashboard and optionally by email.

Proactive Mode sessions process your regulatory exposure profile (sector, compliance risk areas, ACEI category scores) against the live regulatory intelligence pipeline. They do not access the text of your uploaded documents or prior query text. You may disable Proactive Mode at any time by contacting support@ailane.ai or via account settings. Disabling Proactive Mode does not affect your access to Standard Mode or any other contracted feature. See also Section 9.4 and our Privacy Policy (Section 7).
