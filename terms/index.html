SECTION 4

Description of Services

Ailane provides the following core services, the availability of which is dependent on your subscription tier:

  • Compliance Checker: Upload employment contracts, staff handbooks, or workplace policies for automated analysis against the Ailane Knowledge Library of UK legislative instruments. Outputs identify potential compliance issues with statutory references and remediation guidance.
  • Knowledge Library: Access to our curated database of UK employment legislation, statutory instruments, case law summaries, and regulatory updates across 12 employment law categories.
  • Adverse Claim Exposure Index (ACEI): Tribunal claim pattern data and employer exposure scoring based on 130,000+ UK employment tribunal decisions (Governance and Institutional tiers).
  • Regulatory Readiness Index (RRI): Forward-looking organisational preparedness assessment across five compliance pillars (Governance and Institutional tiers).
  • Compliance Conduct Index (CCI): Historical compliance conduct scoring with Bayesian credibility weighting (Governance and Institutional tiers).
  • Regulatory Intelligence Ticker: AI-generated constitutional analysis of regulatory developments relevant to your sector (Governance and Institutional tiers).
  • Flash Check: Single-document one-off compliance analysis available without a subscription, accessible via one-time payment.
