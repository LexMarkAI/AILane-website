### 6. Eileen — automated processing and the absence of automated decisions

The workspace includes **Eileen**, our deal-room intelligence assistant. Eileen is an artificial-intelligence system; her responses are generated using large language models provided by Anthropic (see section 8). She provides regulatory intelligence and contextual information and supports your navigation of the engagement.

Your exchanges with Eileen are recorded and retained so that we can operate the assistant, maintain continuity, monitor and improve quality, and keep an audit trail. Eileen's outputs are regulatory intelligence and contextual information; they are **not legal advice**, are not a substitute for advice from a qualified professional, and are not legally binding instruments.

**No solely automated decision-making.** We do not make decisions about you that produce legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affect you based solely on automated processing, within the meaning of Article 22 UK GDPR. Eileen does not decide your access, your organisation's commercial terms, or any other matter of legal or similar significance; such matters involve human judgement.

### 7. Who operates and can see this workspace

AI Lane Limited operates this workspace and **can access and view its contents**, including the materials shared, the notes and drafts created within it, and the record of interactions with Eileen. Access by our personnel is limited to those who need it to operate, support, secure and audit the service, and is subject to confidentiality obligations.

Some of our processors are located in, or transfer personal data to, the United States. Where we transfer your personal data outside the United Kingdom to a country without UK "adequacy" status, we rely on appropriate safeguards: principally the **UK Extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework** (the UK-US data bridge) where the recipient is certified under it, supported by the **EU Standard Contractual Clauses as amended by the UK International Data Transfer Addendum**, or the **UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA)**. Our primary database storage is located in the EEA (Frankfurt). You may request a copy of the relevant safeguards using the contact details in section 15.

### 10. How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data for the duration of the engagement and thereafter for as long as necessary for our legal, regulatory, contractual and accounting purposes — ordinarily for up to six years following the end of the engagement, in line with applicable limitation periods and our Records of Processing Activities (ROPA-001) — after which it is securely deleted or anonymised. The acceptance record (section 3) is retained for the same period as evidence of the terms accepted. Records may be retained for longer where required by law or to establish, exercise or defend legal claims.

### 11. Your rights

Subject to the conditions and exemptions in UK data-protection law, you have the right to: request **access** to your personal data; request **rectification** of inaccurate data; request **erasure**; request **restriction** of processing; **object** to processing carried out on the basis of legitimate interests; and request **data portability** where applicable. Where processing is based on consent (for example, analytics cookies), you may **withdraw consent** at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any right, contact **privacy@ailane.ai**. We will respond within one month, which may be extended for complex requests, and we will tell you if so.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the **Information Commissioner's Office** (ICO), the UK supervisory authority, at `ico.org.uk` or on 0303 123 1113. We would, however, welcome the chance to address your concerns first.

### 12. How we keep your data secure

We maintain appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including: encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest; row-level security and least-privilege access controls within the platform; authenticated, per-counterparty access scoping; activity and audit logging; and due-diligence and contractual controls over our processors. In the event of a personal data breach, we will assess and, where required, notify the ICO and affected individuals in accordance with Articles 33 and 34 UK GDPR.

### 13. Cookies and similar technologies

The workspace uses **strictly necessary cookies** to authenticate you and maintain your secure session; these are required for the workspace to function and do not require consent. The workspace pages also use **Google Analytics (GA4)** for aggregate usage measurement; analytics cookies are set **only where you have given consent** through the cookie banner, and you may decline or withdraw consent at any time. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies in this workspace. This reflects our obligations under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).

### 14. Changes to this notice

We may update this notice from time to time. The version reference appears at the top of this document, and the current version is always available at **ailane.ai/legal/deal-room-privacy/**. Where a change is material, we will bring it to your attention and, where appropriate, ask you to acknowledge the updated version.
