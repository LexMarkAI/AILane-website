This document has two parts. Part A is the Privacy Notice required by Articles 13 and 14 of the UK GDPR and explains how AI Lane Limited processes personal data within this workspace. Part B sets out the Workspace Terms of Use that govern access to and use of the workspace. By accessing this workspace and confirming acceptance at the entry screen, you acknowledge Part A and agree to Part B.

This notice concerns the personal data of the individuals who access this deal-room workspace (the counterparty's representatives). It is distinct from, and additional to, the public Ailane Privacy Notice at ailane.ai/privacy/ and the Ailane Tribunal Privacy Notice that governs the platform's analytical corpus.

Part A

Part A — Privacy Notice

1. Who we are (the controller)

This workspace is operated by AI Lane Limited ("Ailane", "we", "us", "our"), a company registered in England & Wales with company number 17035654 and registered office in Wales. We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) under registration number 00013389720. We are the controller of the personal data described in this notice.

Privacy contact: privacy@ailane.ai. Commercial contact: partnerships@ailane.ai. Ailane and the Ailane device are trademarks of AI Lane Limited (UK trademark UK00004347220, Class 42).

2. What this workspace is

This deal-room is a secure, access-controlled workspace through which AI Lane Limited shares information with a prospective institutional data partner and progresses a potential commercial engagement. It contains documents, commercial proposals, methodology overviews, data samples, and an interactive intelligence assistant ("Eileen"). Access is restricted to named representatives of the counterparty by authenticated invitation.

We obtain your personal data directly from you (when you use the workspace), from your organisation, and from the individual who initiated or facilitated the engagement (for example, where a colleague provides your name and business email so that access can be provisioned to you).

6. Eileen — automated processing and the absence of automated decisions

The workspace includes Eileen, our deal-room intelligence assistant. Eileen is an artificial-intelligence system; her responses are generated using large language models provided by Anthropic (see section 8). She provides regulatory intelligence and contextual information and supports your navigation of the engagement.

Your exchanges with Eileen are recorded and retained so that we can operate the assistant, maintain continuity, monitor and improve quality, and keep an audit trail. Eileen's outputs are regulatory intelligence and contextual information; they are not legal advice, are not a substitute for advice from a qualified professional, and are not legally binding instruments.

No solely automated decision-making. We do not make decisions about you that produce legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affect you based solely on automated processing, within the meaning of Article 22 UK GDPR. Eileen does not decide your access, your organisation's commercial terms, or any other matter of legal or similar significance; such matters involve human judgement.

7. Who operates and can see this workspace

AI Lane Limited operates this workspace and can access and view its contents, including the materials shared, the notes and drafts created within it, and the record of interactions with Eileen. Access by our personnel is limited to those who need it to operate, support, secure and audit the service, and is subject to confidentiality obligations.

9. International transfers

Some of our processors are located in, or transfer personal data to, the United States. Where we transfer your personal data outside the United Kingdom to a country without UK "adequacy" status, we rely on appropriate safeguards: principally the UK Extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (the UK-US data bridge) where the recipient is certified under it, supported by the EU Standard Contractual Clauses as amended by the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, or the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA). Our primary database storage is located in the EEA (Frankfurt). You may request a copy of the relevant safeguards using the contact details in section 15.

10. How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data for the duration of the engagement and thereafter for as long as necessary for our legal, regulatory, contractual and accounting purposes — ordinarily for up to six years following the end of the engagement, in line with applicable limitation periods and our Records of Processing Activities (ROPA-001) — after which it is securely deleted or anonymised. The acceptance record (section 3) is retained for the same period as evidence of the terms accepted. Records may be retained for longer where required by law or to establish, exercise or defend legal claims.

11. Your rights

Subject to the conditions and exemptions in UK data-protection law, you have the right to: request access to your personal data; request rectification of inaccurate data; request erasure; request restriction of processing; object to processing carried out on the basis of legitimate interests; and request data portability where applicable. Where processing is based on consent (for example, analytics cookies), you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any right, contact privacy@ailane.ai. We will respond within one month, which may be extended for complex requests, and we will tell you if so.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK supervisory authority, at ico.org.uk or on 0303 123 1113. We would, however, welcome the chance to address your concerns first.

12. How we keep your data secure

We maintain appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including: encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest; row-level security and least-privilege access controls within the platform; authenticated, per-counterparty access scoping; activity and audit logging; and due-diligence and contractual controls over our processors. In the event of a personal data breach, we will assess and, where required, notify the ICO and affected individuals in accordance with Articles 33 and 34 UK GDPR.

13. Cookies and similar technologies

The workspace uses strictly necessary cookies to authenticate you and maintain your secure session; these are required for the workspace to function and do not require consent. The workspace pages also use Google Analytics (GA4) for aggregate usage measurement; analytics cookies are set only where you have given consent through the cookie banner, and you may decline or withdraw consent at any time. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies in this workspace. This reflects our obligations under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).

14. Changes to this notice

We may update this notice from time to time. The version reference appears at the top of this document, and the current version is always available at ailane.ai/legal/deal-room-privacy/. Where a change is material, we will bring it to your attention and, where appropriate, ask you to acknowledge the updated version.

15. Contact

For any question about this notice or about how we handle your personal data, contact our privacy team at privacy@ailane.ai.

Part B — Workspace Terms of Use

You confirm that you are authorised to access the workspace on behalf of the counterparty organisation and to accept these terms in connection with the prospective engagement.

They are without prejudice to, and subordinate to, any Non-Disclosure Agreement, Master Services Agreement, Data Processing Agreement or other definitive agreement executed between AI Lane Limited and the counterparty. In the event of any conflict, such executed agreements prevail for their subject matter.

3. Nature of the workspace — intelligence, not legal advice. The workspace, its materials, and the Eileen assistant provide regulatory intelligence and contextual information. They do not constitute legal advice, do not certify compliance, and are not legally binding instruments. Outputs are designed to inform your own assessment and are not a substitute for the judgement of a qualified legal professional. You should obtain independent professional advice before acting on any matter arising in the workspace.

4. Permitted use. You may access and use the workspace solely to evaluate and progress the prospective engagement with AI Lane Limited. Any other use is not permitted.

5. Confidentiality. The contents of the workspace — including documents, commercial proposals and pricing, methodology and index descriptions, data samples, and the substance of interactions with Eileen — are confidential to AI Lane Limited. You must keep them confidential, must not disclose them to any third party except as expressly permitted or as provided under an executed Non-Disclosure Agreement, and must not copy or extract them except as necessary for the permitted use.

6. Intellectual property. AI Lane Limited and its licensors own all intellectual property rights in the workspace, the platform, the Eileen assistant, the data estate, the ACEI, RRI and CCI indices and methodologies, and all materials made available. "Ailane" and the Ailane device are trademarks of AI Lane Limited. Nothing in these terms transfers any such rights to you; you are granted only a limited, revocable right to access and use the workspace for the permitted use. You must not scrape, harvest, bulk-extract, reverse-engineer, or use any workspace content to train or develop any machine-learning model.

7. Acceptable use. You must not use the workspace for any unlawful purpose; must not attempt to circumvent, disable or probe its access controls or security; must not share your access link or credentials with any other person; and must not use any automated means to access or extract its contents.

8. Operation and monitoring. AI Lane Limited operates the workspace, can access and view its contents, and logs activity for the purposes of operating, securing and auditing the service, as described in Part A.

9. Suspension and termination. AI Lane Limited may suspend or withdraw your access to the workspace at any time, including on conclusion of the engagement or in response to a breach of these terms. 10. Availability. The workspace is made available for the purpose of the prospective engagement. We do not warrant that it will be uninterrupted or error-free, and we may modify, suspend or discontinue any part of it.

11. Liability. Nothing in these terms excludes or limits any liability that cannot lawfully be excluded or limited. Subject to that, the workspace and its intelligence are provided to inform your own assessment and do not guarantee any particular outcome, and our liability arising out of or in connection with the workspace is limited as set out in any executed agreement between the parties or, in the absence of such agreement, to the maximum extent permitted by law.

13. Governing law and jurisdiction. These terms, and any dispute or claim arising out of or in connection with them, are governed by the laws of England & Wales, and the courts of England & Wales have exclusive jurisdiction.

14. Variation. We may update these terms from time to time. Where a change is material, we will bring it to your attention; your continued use of the workspace after a change takes effect constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

15. Contact. Questions about these terms may be directed to partnerships@ailane.ai; questions about personal data to privacy@ailane.ai.
